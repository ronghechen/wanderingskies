Wandering Skies

Controls for camera movement: WASD forward/backward/left/right, R to go up, and F to go down

Press 1 for daytime (default), 2 for sunset, and 3 for night

  • W - move forward
  • A - move left
  • S - move backward
  • D - move right
  • R - go up
  • F - go down
  • Up arrow - tilt camera up
  • Down arrow - tilt camera down
  • T - Toggle between Toon (purple tint) and Phong shading
